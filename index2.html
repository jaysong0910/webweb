My Autobiography

The Man with full of courage Jayson Llacuna Garcia, was born on the 10th Day of September 2002 in San Agustin, Isabela but currently living at Sto. Domingo, Luna, Isabela and have three siblings- 1 brother and 2sisters they are also helping him in his growth as their parents’ does. In his stage of growing, he had experienced different types of bullying; physical, emotional and in cyber, but bullying doesn’t stop him for doing what is right and good things to others but he is taking it as an additional strengths in making his dreams come true. As how he explained his daily life, he has God on his side, Our Almighty God is helping him to walk uprightly and faithfully everyday, as what the Bible says “Nothing is Impossible”, that all things can be possible if you believe in the name of Jesus and all things can be better, we just need to pray, doing our best all the time and thanking Him for all he has done and to all the things he will going to do for me, to you and to all of us.
